Learning Goals

AI Builder is a user-friendly tool that allows you to easily create custom Artificial Intelligence (AI) models to automate processes, gain insights, and improve decision-making. It is designed to be accessible to users without extensive technical expertise, and integrates seamlessly with other Microsoft products like Power Apps and Power Automate. In this course, you will learn how to train and use AI models and integrate these with your own Power Apps or Power Automate flows.

Microsoft Syntex integrates AI in SharePoint. You will learn how to extract information and insights from unstructured data and documents by using the Microsoft Syntex capabilities.

Target Audience

This course is targeted at IT Professionals and Business users with usage knowledge of Power Apps, Power Automate and SharePoint. They want to learn how to extend the functionalities in the Power Platform & SharePoint with the help of Artificial Intelligence.
